Office System Upgrade

Last Friday, the office was closed due to a system upgrade that had been planned for several months. Employees in the IT department tested the new software, which was designed to improve data security. All staff were asked to complete their tasks in advance. The upgrade finished earlier than expected, and normal operations resumed the following Monday.

1. Why was the office closed?

正確答案：Because of a system upgrade
解析：文章第一句 "the office was closed due to a system upgrade" 明確指出關閉原因。其他選項都未在文章中提及，如員工休假或停電，因此不正確。

2. Who tested the new software?

正確答案：IT department
解析：文章說 "Employees in the IT department tested the new software"。注意不是所有員工，而是特定部門。其他選項未提及。

Department Transfer

Ms. Carter, who had worked at the company for over five years, decided to transfer to another department last month. During her absence, Mr. Lee took over her duties. Documents completed during this period were reviewed by the team leader to ensure all procedures were correctly followed.

3. What decision did Ms. Carter make?

正確答案：She changed departments
解析："decided to transfer to another department" 是關鍵短語，明確表示她改調部門，而非離職或休假。

4. What happened to the documents?

正確答案：They were reviewed by the team leader
解析：文章明確說 "Documents completed during this period were reviewed by the team leader"，指出被主管審核以確保流程正確。

Training Program Update

Last year, the company introduced a comprehensive staff training program aimed at improving communication skills, email etiquette, teamwork, and problem-solving strategies. Employees attended weekly sessions that included interactive exercises, role-playing scenarios, and detailed feedback. Participants were required to complete all assignments on time. The program encouraged peer learning and discussion to share experiences and best practices. After completing the program, employees reported increased confidence, fewer mistakes in client communication, improved coordination among teams, and faster handling of tasks. Supervisors also observed better performance metrics and higher morale across the department. This training initiative has since become a model for other departments and is considered an important part of ongoing professional development within the company.

5. When was the training program introduced?

正確答案：Last year
解析：文章開頭 "Last year, the company introduced..." 清楚指出時間為去年。其他時間選項與文章無關。

6. Who must complete all assignments?

正確答案：Employees participating in the program
解析：文章說 "Participants were required to complete all assignments"，明確指參加員工，而非其他群體。

7. What was the result after the program?

正確答案：Improved performance and teamwork
解析：文章描述 "employees reported increased confidence, fewer mistakes... improved coordination..."，明確表明績效提升和團隊合作改善。

Communication Workshop

Next Saturday, the company will hold an extensive communication workshop for employees. The three-hour session will cover professional email writing, meeting strategies, cross-cultural collaboration, and conflict resolution. Participants will engage in role-plays, group discussions, interactive exercises, and receive personalized feedback from trainers. Employees working in international teams are expected to benefit the most. The workshop aims to improve clarity in communication, reduce misunderstandings, boost confidence in presentations, and enhance teamwork. It also provides practical advice for composing reports, handling client inquiries, and collaborating across departments. Management considers this workshop a key initiative for professional development and overall organizational efficiency.

8. What is the main focus of the workshop?

正確答案：Communication at work, including emails and meetings
解析：文章多次提到提升工作溝通，包括 email、會議和跨部門協作，明確表明焦點是職場溝通。

9. How long is the workshop?

正確答案：Three hours
解析：文章說明 "The three-hour session..."，直接指出時長。

10. Who benefits most from the workshop?

正確答案：Employees in international teams
解析：文章明確指出 "Employees working in international teams are expected to benefit the most"。
