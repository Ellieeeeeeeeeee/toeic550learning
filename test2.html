Reading Practice

Office System Upgrade

Last Friday, the office was closed due to a system upgrade that had been planned for several months. Employees who worked in the IT department were in charge of testing the new software, which was designed to improve data security. As a result, all staff members were asked to complete their tasks in advance. The upgrade was finished earlier than expected, and normal operations resumed the following Monday.

1. Why was the office closed?

正確答案：Because a system upgrade took place
解析：due to 表示「因為」，後面接名詞，說明辦公室關閉的原因。

2. Who tested the new software?

正確答案：The IT department
解析：be in charge of 表示「負責」，主詞是 IT department。

Department Transfer

Ms. Carter, who had worked at the company for over five years, decided to transfer to another department last month. During her absence, Mr. Lee took over her duties. Any documents that were completed during this period were reviewed by the team leader, who ensured that all procedures were followed correctly.

3. What decision did Ms. Carter make?

正確答案：She changed departments
解析：transfer to another department 表示「調到其他部門」。

4. What happened to the documents?

正確答案：They were reviewed by the team leader
解析：were reviewed 是被動語態，表示文件「被審核」。

Training Program Update

The training program that was introduced last year has been improved based on employee feedback. Workers who take part in the program are required to complete all assignments by the deadline. Since the changes were made, the department where the training takes place has reported higher performance.

5. When was the training program introduced?

正確答案：Last year
解析：明確過去時間，搭配 was introduced。

6. Who must complete all assignments?

正確答案：Employees who join the program
解析：who 是關係代名詞，用來修飾 employees。

7. What was the result after the changes?

正確答案：Performance improved
解析：has reported higher performance 表示績效提升。

Communication Workshop

A workshop on workplace communication will be held next Saturday. Participants who attend the event will learn practical expressions and useful phrases that are often used in business meetings. The session, which will last three hours, is expected to benefit employees who work in international teams.

8. What is the workshop mainly about?

正確答案：Communication at work
解析：第一句直接說明 workshop 主題。

9. How long will the session last?

正確答案：Three hours
解析：which will last three hours 是關係子句。

10. Who will benefit most from the workshop?

正確答案：Employees in international teams
解析：文章最後一句直接點出受益對象。
