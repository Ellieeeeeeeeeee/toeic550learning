Reading Practice

Text A: Office Announcement

Last Friday, the office was closed due to a system upgrade that had been planned for several months. Employees who worked in the IT department were in charge of testing the new software, which was designed to improve data security. As a result, all staff members were asked to complete their tasks in advance.

1. Why was the office closed?

正確答案：Because a system upgrade took place
解析：due to 表示「因為」，說明關閉原因。

2. Who was responsible for testing the software?

正確答案：The IT department
解析：in charge of 表示「負責」。

Text B: Business Email

Ms. Carter, who had worked at the company for over five years, decided to transfer to another department last month. During her absence, Mr. Lee was assigned to take over her duties. Any documents that were completed during this period were reviewed by the team leader.

3. What decision did Ms. Carter make?

正確答案：She changed departments
解析：transfer 表示「調部門」。

4. What happened to the documents?

正確答案：They were reviewed by the team leader
解析：被動語態：were reviewed。

Text C: Company Policy Update

The training program that was introduced last year has been improved based on employee feedback. Workers who take part in the program are required to complete all assignments by the deadline. The department where the training takes place has reported higher performance since the changes were made.

5. When was the training program introduced?

正確答案：Last year
解析：明確過去時間，使用過去式。

6. Who must complete the assignments?

正確答案：Employees who join the program
解析：who 是關係代名詞，修飾 employees。

7. What was the result of the changes?

正確答案：Performance improved
解析：higher performance 表示成效提升。

Text D: Event Information

A workshop on workplace communication will be held next Saturday. Participants who attend the event will learn practical expressions and useful phrases that are often used in business meetings. The session, which will last three hours, is expected to benefit employees who work in international teams.

8. What is the workshop about?

正確答案：Communication at work
解析：第一句直接點出主題。

9. How long will the session last?

正確答案：Three hours
解析：which will last three hours 為關係子句。

10. Who will benefit most from this workshop?

正確答案：Employees in international teams
解析：benefit employees who work in international teams。
